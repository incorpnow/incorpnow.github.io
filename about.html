Introducing InCorpNow

Welcome to InCorpNow,

a team of experienced Lawyers, Chartered Accountants (CAs), and Business Consultants providing complete legal, financial, and business compliance solutions under one roof. At InCorpNow, we specialize in helping startups, entrepreneurs, and established businesses navigate every stage of their compliance and regulatory journey — from formation to growth. Our experts ensure that your business remains legally sound, financially compliant, and strategically positioned for success.

Our range of services includes:

  • Business Formation: Incorporation of Private Limited Companies, LLPs, Partnerships, NGOs, Trusts, and Societies
  • Compliance & Governance: Annual filings, ROC compliance, legal drafting, and secretarial services
  • Taxation & Finance: GST registration and returns, Income Tax filings, accounting, and advisory
  • Intellectual Property: Trademark registration, objection reply and drafting, renewals, and IP consultancy
  • Licenses & Registrations: Assistance with FSSAI (Food License), Drug License, Import-Export Code (IEC), Trade License, and other regulatory approvals
  • Business Consultancy: Guidance on restructuring, due diligence, and compliance management

At InCorpNow, we believe in building long-term partnerships based on trust, transparency, and timely service delivery. Our mission is to simplify complex legal and compliance processes so you can focus on what truly matters — growing your business.
